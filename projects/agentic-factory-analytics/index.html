Overview

Part 2 of the factory data initiative at Somic Ishikawa. Builds on the Industrial Edge Data Platform, where granular shop-floor data is persisted in PostgreSQL after TRITON edge ingestion and n8n + FastAPI processing.

This layer is for AI and agentic analytics — enabling the agentic team to query, reason over, and act on owned factory data without the register and license limits of the legacy IoT stack. Technical details, architecture, and outcomes will be published here as the case study is completed.

Overview

Supported a healthcare analytics product used by business and operations teams to manage and analyze clinical, claims, insurance, and billing data. Worked across development, QA, and operations to maintain data accuracy and delivery reliability.

Platform Capabilities

Analytics support across clinical, claims, and billing domains with reliable ETL and cross-team delivery.

Clinical / claims / billing analytics support

Enabled business and operations teams to work with clinical, claims, insurance, and billing data through accurate, queryable analytics layers.

Cross-team requirement gathering

Collaborated with development, QA, and operations to capture new data requirements while protecting existing database entities.

Root-cause data debugging

Improved data accuracy through systematic root-cause analysis on production data issues and migration debugging.

ETL pipeline reliability

Maintained dependable ETL data flows — reducing issue resolution time and improving delivery predictability for downstream consumers.

Challenges Tackled

Data platform constraints common in regulated healthcare analytics environments.

New requirements without breaking existing DB entities

Business teams needed expanded analytics without schema regressions — requiring careful requirement scoping and backward-compatible data modeling.

Data migration debugging

Migration issues surfaced as subtle data drift in production — resolved through targeted debugging rather than broad re-runs that risked downtime.

Cross-team communication latency

Handoffs between dev, QA, and ops slowed issue resolution — streamlined through structured communication workflows and clearer ownership.

  • Improved delivery predictability through structured communication workflows
